Steady state solutions

In this section, hytool provides a few analytical solutions for steady state flow regime. These solutions are more used for estimating discharge to wells in these conditions rather than to interpret pumping tests.

thiem Estimates T with Thiem's formula for steady state flow in an ideal confined aquifer for two observations
thm Estimates T with Thiem's formula for steady state flow in an ideal confined or unconfined aquifer for two and more observations
deg Estimates T and c with de Glee's formula for steady state flow in an ideal half-confined aquifer for two and more observations
thc_std Computes the discharge for a well located close to a constant head boundary with the Dupuit's formula
lei_std Computes the discharge for a well located close to a constant head boundary with the solution of
tcn_std Computes the discharge for a well located between a constant head and a no flow boundaries
